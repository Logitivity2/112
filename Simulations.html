The Simulated Problem Solving Modules are split into 2 Streams: Mathematical Reasoning & Scientific Reasoning.

The Simulated Exercises give practical meaning to abstract concepts and
engage learners to reason from first principles in the fields of Mathematics & Physics.

Although the modules may be accessed independently, it is best to attempt them according to their numbering.

For ages 13 & up.
